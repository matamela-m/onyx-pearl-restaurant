Our Story

Born from a singular passion for the art of dining

Our Philosophy

Where Craft
Becomes Memory

Onyx & Pearl was born from a single, unwavering belief: that the act of dining can be transformative. Not merely satisfying; but truly moving. A conversation between chef and guest, conducted entirely through flavour, texture, and the quiet theatre of a perfectly composed plate.

We opened our doors in 2018 with fourteen seats and one vision: to create a restaurant where nothing is rushed, nothing is accidental, and every moment is charged with intention. Six years later, that vision remains unchanged, even as our craft has deepened.

Our ingredients are sourced with obsessive care: day-boat fish from trusted harbour communities, heritage breed animals raised on open pasture, vegetables harvested at the peak of their season. We believe that great cooking begins long before the kitchen.

01
Provenance Above All
We know the name of every farmer, fisherman, and forager who contributes to our menu.

Technique in Service of Flavour
We deploy classical and contemporary techniques only when they deepen the experience — never for their own sake.

Hospitality as an Art Form
Every member of our team considers their role an act of genuine care — for the guest, the food, and the moment.

Meet the Chef

Chef
Michael Tshabalala

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Michael Tshabalala grew up in a small coastal village in Arniston, South Africa, where his grandmother's kitchen was both his classroom and his sanctuary. He would later train under the great kitchens of Paris, Kyoto, and Copenhagen: absorbing not just technique, but a philosophy of patience and restraint.

After a decade of working in some of the world's most celebrated restaurants, Michael returned to his roots: not to replicate what he had learned, but to synthesise it into something entirely his own. In 2018, he co-founded Onyx & Pearl with one ambition: to build a restaurant worthy of a lifetime.

The most important ingredient in any kitchen is time. Time to understand your produce, time to listen to the seasons, time to be still enough to hear what a dish needs to become.

Michael Tshabalala

Our Journey

Six Years of
Becoming

2018

The First Table

Onyx & Pearl opens with 14 seats in the Artisan Quarter. Fully booked for its first six months within two weeks of announcement.

2020

The Michelin Recognition

Included in the Michelin Guide for the first time. Chef Michael dedicates the recognition to his grandmother in Arniston.

2022

The Private Dining Room

We expand into an adjacent space, creating The Pearl Chamber: an intimate private dining room for up to 12 guests.

2023

James Beard Finalist

Named a James Beard Award finalist in the Best New Restaurant category: recognising not just food, but the complete experience we have built.

2025

Today — Still Becoming

Our journey continues. Each season brings a new chapter: new produce, new ideas, and a renewed commitment to the singular experience of a great meal shared between people.
